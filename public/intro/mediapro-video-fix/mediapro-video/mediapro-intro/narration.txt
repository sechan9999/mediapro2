Every day, millions of people hit Record. Then wait for installs. Wait for uploads. Wait for their privacy to be handed over. What if you didn't have to?

Introducing Media Hub Pro. A browser-based screen recording suite that runs entirely in your browser. No installs. No accounts. No uploads. Everything you capture stays on your machine. Always.

Hit Record to capture your screen in stunning clarity. Up to 4K at 60 frames per second. Or choose Go Live to stream directly from the browser. Pick your preset. Resolution, frame rate, bitrate. All in one click.

Professional voiceover means balancing two audio sources at once. Media Hub Pro gives you live gain control over your microphone and system audio, independently. Mute either track on the fly, mid-session.

Ideas move fast. So should your markup. Draw directly on your capture with the Pen tool. Highlight key moments. Drop a callout to focus attention exactly where it needs to be. Five colors. Real-time canvas.

When done, trim your clip right in the browser using FFmpeg WebAssembly. No server, no upload, no wait. Opt-in to Cloud Export and push your recording to Google Drive or Dropbox with a single click.

Need a second opinion before you publish? Generate a shareable review link. No cloud account needed. No sign-up for your reviewer. Just a link, good for 24 hours.

Media Hub Pro detects your device automatically. On phones and tablets, the app tells you exactly what works and gracefully guides you to the best experience. Desktop power. Mobile aware.

Record. Stream. Annotate. Share. All in your browser. All on your terms. Media Hub Pro. Built for creators who don't compromise.
